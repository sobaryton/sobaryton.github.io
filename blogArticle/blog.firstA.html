Career change? Are you crazy or what?

Do you know this feeling, that everything goes wrong and you loose your smile, your motivation until you don't even want to go to work? Maybe it's time for you to move on.

This is my story, but I am sure that you can find lots of other stories too. For me, everything began in Jan 2017. At the time I was working in the food industry in the quality department, which is in charge of the product safety. The work was quite repetitive and I needed challenges. That is why I left my job and just concentrate on myself and what I wanted to do. This was really hard decision for me, but really needed.

I am living with my partner, who is an experienced developer, more specialised in back-end development. We know eachother since university.

I worked three years for the food industry in the technical department in France and UK. These experiences especially brought me stress-control, time management and accuracy. In January 2017 I decided to change my career path and my whole life to become front-end developer.

Why? Because I need a work environment more challenging day-to-day and with a touch of creativity. I discovered the web development, especially the front end development and I enjoyed it quickly.

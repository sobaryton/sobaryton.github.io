About me

Solene Bary, 25 years old
Webdesigner, junior - London


I worked three years for food industry in the technical department. These experiences especially brought me stress-control, time management and accuracy. In January 2017 I decided to change my career plan and my whole life to become webdesigner.

Why? Because I need a work environment more challenging day-to-day and with a touch of creativity. I discovered the web development, especially the front end development and I enjoyed it quickly. I developed my development skills with OpenClassRooms, which is an e-learning platform recognized in France and UK. As a fast learner and having thirsty of knowledge, in three months I got 13 certificates in total: 7 in front-end development, 5 in design.

Have a look and go to see my work in the work section to have an idea of my projects.

My goal now: be happy at work and still learn more about web-development.
So if you are interested to help me growing, don't hesitate to contact me.
